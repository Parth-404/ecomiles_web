📡 Eco Facts
How it works

Three Steps to Greener Travel

No accounts needed to start. Just pick your transport and go.

🚀

1. Pick Your Mode

Choose from 8 transport options — bike, walk, bus, train, car (petrol/diesel/electric), or plane.

📐

2. Enter Details

Add distance, number of passengers, and whether it's a round trip. We handle the maths.

🌱

3. Get Insights

See CO₂ totals, eco score, trees needed, fuel cost estimates, and personalised green tips.

Features

Everything You Need

Built for curious travellers who care about their planet.

🎯

Eco Score

A/B/C grade system that rates your trip's environmental impact instantly.

📊

Visual Charts

Animated bar charts comparing your choice against all other modes.

🌳

Tree Counter

See exactly how many trees it takes to offset your trip's emissions.

⚖️

Side-by-Side Compare

Compare any two transport modes head-to-head for the same journey.

📝

Trip History Log

Save trips, view cumulative CO₂, and track your eco progress over time.

💡

Smart Equivalences

CO₂ in real-world terms: smartphones charged, litres of water boiled, and more.

🔄

Round Trip Toggle

One-way or return trip — automatically doubles the distance and recalculates.

📤

Share Results

Copy or share your eco report to spread awareness with friends and family.
